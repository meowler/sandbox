Lindsey Fowler
  • Lucky Duck
  • 01
  • The design brief for Lucky Duck provided a vague description of a kid’s clothing store located in Brooklyn, New York. There were many unknowns but the first objective was to create the logo. I was challenged with infinite solutions to illustrate “duck”. I discovered by representing the logo with an egg made from fabric and thread aligns with Lucky Duck being an apparel store for children. The final egg is made from soft felt and sewn with red thread using a blanket stitch.
